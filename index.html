Endalkachew Abebe Kebede

I am a hydrologist and environmental data scientist dedicated to advancing the sustainability of global freshwater and food systems. My research integrates hydrological modeling, remote sensing, and geospatial data science to understand how the spatial and temporal dynamics of irrigation, land use, and climate reshape surface and groundwater resources. By coupling Earth observation, crop–water modeling, and data analytics, I aim to quantify hydrological change and identify pathways toward resilient and sustainable water systems.
