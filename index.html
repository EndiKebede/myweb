Welcome

I am an environmental data scientist and hydrologist focused on sustainable water and food systems. My work connects Earth observation, hydrological & crop–water modeling, and policy-relevant analysis to understand how irrigation, climate, and socioeconomic constraints shape water scarcity and resilience.
