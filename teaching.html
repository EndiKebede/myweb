Teaching

I teach quantitative and geospatial approaches for environmental problem-solving, with an emphasis on reproducible analysis, hands-on learning, and connecting methods to real-world water and food challenges.

Teaching focus

  • Geospatial analysis & GIS workflows
  • Hydrology, irrigation engineering, and water resources
  • Environmental data analysis and reproducibility
  • Applied learning through real datasets and case studies
